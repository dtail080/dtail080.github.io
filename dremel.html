Dremel 3D Tech Specs

The Dremel 3D printer is ideal for getting a 3D print done fast but quality is not the most important factor.

Pros

  • Fastest printer in the uOttawa Makerspace
  • Reliably Finishes Prints
  • Easy to transfer files to the printer by using either USB or SD card

Cons

  • High quality on the dremel is similar to standard quality on our other 3D printers
  • No heated build-plate
  • Slicer does not have built in support generator, but supports can be added using Meshmixer
