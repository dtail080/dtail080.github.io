Benefits of being a volunteer

  • Gain valuable experience with technology
  • Meet people with similar interests
  • Collaborate on cool new projects
  • Get involved with the Maker network

Volunteer Opportunites

  • Help out during workshops (2-3 times a week)
  • Help out with on campus events such as Design day, makerspace challenges, clubs day, entrepreneurship week
  • Help out with off campus events such as makermobile outings, conferences, festivals
  • Help maintain equipment
  • Provide training for those who wish to learn more about the technology in the Makerspace
  • Create and design projects requested by the community

How to become a volunteer

Get trained:

Become familiar with all the platforms available tin the makerspace

  • Soldering station
  • Laser cutter
  • 3D printing on all

Get Certified

To get certified, the volunteer must have a standard knowledge of the equipment. Receive training from a Makerspace coordinator and wait up to 24 hrs before returning to perform their certification test.
